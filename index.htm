How to play

Controls

Use arrow keys or [W,A,S,D] to guide the Agent.

To pause / resume the game press [SPACE] or [ESC] or just click into the game area.

Controls

Use swipe gestures to guide the Agent.

Alternatively use the Arrow Buttons underneath the game area to navigate.

To pause / resume the game, touch the game area once.

Trackers

Trackers are entities trying to deanonymize the Agent if they catch him.

Each tracker has its own way of chasing the Agent.

Inky

Inky will stay in the ghost house until the Agent has collected at least 30 fragments. His home is the bottom right corner.

Blinky

Blinky is the most agressive of the 4 trackers. He will start chasing the Agent right away, and aim directly at him. His home is the upper right corner.

Pinky

Pinky will start chasing the Agent right away, he will always aim 4 fields ahead and 4 fields left of the Agent. His home is the upper left corner.

Clyde

Clyde will stay in the ghost house until the Agent has collected at least 2/3 of all fragments. His home is the bottom left corner.

Tracker moods

The trackers have two different moods that change the way they act during the game.

Scatter mood

This is the default mood. When trackers are in scatter mood, they will just go to their home corner and stay there.

Chase mood

After a certain time the trackers change their mood and want to go chasing the Agent. This is indicated through the walls turning red.

Items

Fragments

The goal of every level is to collect all fragments without being caught by the trackers. Each fragment grants 10 knowledge.

Confidential Mode

In every level there are 4 orbs to activate Confidential Mode, which are a bit bigger than the regular fragments. If the Agent activates those, trackers become vulnerable (they turn blue). Activating it grants 50 knowledge.

Catching a tracker yields 100 knowledge. The tracker will return to the house before starting to chase the Agent again.
